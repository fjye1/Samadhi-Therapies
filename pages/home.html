About

I’m Saba and my path into holistic therapy began long before I ever trained professionally. After stepping away from a life that no longer felt aligned, I travelled across the world from East Africa to the Amazon rainforest volunteering, learning, and immersing myself in cultures where healing is woven into daily life. Along the way, I was introduced to holistic practices and ancestral ways of understanding the body that shifted something deep within me.

My work is rooted in the belief that the body holds stories, and touch can help us listen. I offer holistic treatments creating a gentle space for you to slow down and return to yourself. Each session is an invitation to soften the edges of your day, to breathe a little deeper, and to reconnect with the quiet places within. Whether you’re seeking calm, comfort, or simply a moment of stillness, I hold space with presence, care, and intention.
